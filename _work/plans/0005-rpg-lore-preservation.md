# RPG Lore Preservation Plan

## Purpose

Preserve and modernize the Pitchfork RPG narrative while separating it from the architectural documentation.

The RPG is not merely flavor text. It records the sequence of conceptual discoveries that led to the modern Pancakes and Pitchfork architecture.

The goal is to preserve that journey, not to rewrite history or replace it with the current implementation.

---

# Goals

## Preserve the intellectual progression

The narrative should continue to teach the successive discoveries made by the students.

Examples include:

* colored magic
* tokens
* Blanken
* value
* contracts
* commodities
* Ether
* smart contracts
* restoring balance

Each discovery should build naturally on the previous one.

---

## Separate lore from architecture

The RPG should illustrate the architecture.

It should not define it.

The architecture documents remain the canonical technical specification.

The RPG provides one narrative interpretation.

---

## Modernize the destination

The original storyline culminated in blockchain and smart contracts.

The modern ecosystem is broader.

The ending should evolve toward:

* community-governed civic infrastructure
* stewardship
* local-first computing
* flourishing
* balanced relationships between worlds

The earlier chapters should remain substantially intact.

# Chapter Plan

## Chapter 1

Colored Tokens

Students discover magical colors.

Magic appears to reside in colored tokens.

Everything seems simple.

---

## Chapter 2

Blanken

Students discover Blanken.

Tokens lose color.

Gray tokens remain.

Value is released.

Major revelation:

Token

≠

Value.

---

## Chapter 3

Gray Tokens And Value

Students begin studying value itself.

Magic becomes abstract.

Storage and meaning become separate concepts.

---

## Chapter 4

Contracts

Students discover that promises possess magical structure.

Agreements become magical artifacts.

Contracts begin coordinating value.

---

## Chapter 5

Commodities

Students discover relationships between:

* goods
* labor
* value
* symbolic meaning

Commodity magic emerges.

The economy becomes part of the magical world.

---

## Chapter 6

Ether

Students discover the shared medium connecting worlds.

The Ether explains:

* communication
* transmission
* continuity

The three worlds become visibly connected.

---

## Chapter 7

Smart Contracts

Students realize:

Contracts may execute automatically.

The blockchain metaphor appears here.

Modernize this chapter.

Explain that blockchain was one historical expression of a broader idea:

Community-governed executable agreements.

The chapter should transition toward civic infrastructure rather than ending with cryptocurrency.

---

## Chapter 8

Restoring Balance

The climax.

Students realize:

Magic

Commerce

Ordinary Life

are projections of the same underlying reality.

The goal becomes restoring healthy relationships rather than maximizing magical power.

This chapter should naturally connect to:

* stewardship
* flourishing
* civic institutions
* community governance
* Pancakes

# Preservation Rules

Preserve:

* discovery order
* major revelations
* terminology
* mentor traditions
* emotional progression

Modernize:

* implementation references
* blockchain conclusions
* technology assumptions

Do not rewrite earlier discoveries simply because later architecture evolved.

---

# Relationship To Current Architecture

Architecture explains:

* frequencies
* symbolic crafting
* place
* capabilities
* reference services

The RPG explains how characters gradually discover those ideas.

Neither depends upon the other.

Both remain canonical within their respective domains.

---

# Future Expansion

New chapters may eventually explore:

* place
* stewardship
* reference services
* symbolic conservation
* node capabilities
* flourishing

These should appear as later discoveries rather than revisions of earlier chapters.

The academy should continue learning alongside the ecosystem.

---

# RPG Preservation Copypasta

Use the following guidance whenever updating RPG lore:

> Preserve the narrative sequence:
>
> Colored Tokens → Blanken → Gray Tokens and Released Value → Contracts → Commodities → Ether → Smart Contracts → Restored Balance.
>
> Preserve the discoveries rather than rewriting them.
>
> The RPG is the historical and educational narrative of the ecosystem, not the technical specification.
>
> Modernize only where necessary to align the destination with the current Pancakes philosophy. In particular, evolve the original blockchain ending toward community-governed, privacy-respecting civic infrastructure, stewardship, and flourishing without altering the sequence of conceptual discoveries that precede it.

---

# Editorial Rules

Do not merge lore into implementation documents.

Do not make architecture depend upon the RPG.

Do not reduce the RPG to a mechanics reference.

Treat the lore as an independent narrative that preserves the intellectual history of the project while continuing to inspire future exploration.
