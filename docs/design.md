## Introduction

Embark on a captivating journey into the world of Pitchfork, where the harmony of magic and commerce intertwines to create an enchanting adventure. Explore the depths of tokens, contracts, commodities, and the merging realms as you navigate challenges and uncover hidden truths.

## Core Gameplay Concepts

### Tokens and Color Magic

Tokens are the foundation of Pitchfork's magical gameplay. They immerse players in a world of vibrant colors, each representing a distinct skill category. As you progress, you'll collect tokens of varying colors—Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, and Purple—signifying mastery over different magical abilities. These colors aren't just static markers; they offer a dynamic system where their combination becomes the key to crafting unique spells.

### Contracts and Questgivers

Contracts add a layer of strategic depth to the gameplay. As a questgiver, you create contracts, specifying the rewards for successful completion. These rewards can range from colored tokens to Essence Tokens, offering a wide range of options for both questgivers and quest-takers. This mechanism enables flexibility and fosters a dynamic player-driven economy.

### Blankening Tokens

The "blanken" spell is a powerful tool for releasing the magical energy stored within colored tokens. This process, known as blankening, converts the tokens into gray form. Gray tokens represent a state where the magical essence has been released, rendering them ready to be imbued with new attributes. This transformation plays a crucial role in creating potent spells and contributing to the economic aspects of the game.

### Essence Tokens: Universal Currency

Essence Tokens are at the core of mystical transactions. Imbued with the magical energy released during blankening, these gray tokens serve as the universal magical currency. Players utilize Essence Tokens to fuel spells, create contracts, and engage in market transactions. This universal value unifies the worlds of magic and commerce, forming the backbone of Pitchfork's unique mechanics.

### Commodities and Conversion

Discover a unique facet of the game by exchanging your Essence Tokens for fractional ownership of virtual commodities. These commodities, like wheat, coffee, and more, hold magical energy that can be reabsorbed into your skills through conversion. This introduces an economic dimension to the gameplay, allowing you to strategically manage your resources and investments.

### Scores and Progress Tracking

Throughout your journey, Pitchfork keeps track of your achievements and progress through various scores. These scores include the Skill Enhancement Score, Contract Completion Score, Commodity Conversion Score, Market Success Score, Quester Score, and Questgiver Score. Each score reflects a specific aspect of your gameplay performance, providing a comprehensive view of your magical and economic prowess.

## World Lore

Experience the intertwined realms of Pitchfork:

### The Magical Realm

The magical realm is a harmonious society fueled by the power of six distinct skill categories, each associated with a vibrant color. These categories encompass a wide range of magical abilities, forming the cornerstone of the realm's expertise. The harmony of these colors weaves through every facet of life, offering players a unique and dynamic experience.

### The Parallel World of Commerce

Beyond the magical realm lies a parallel world dominated by commerce. Here, contracts hold sway, offering power through compelling agreements. While this world lacks traditional magic, its contract-based economy introduces a new form of influence. The convergence of these two worlds creates an intricate tapestry of magic and commerce.
