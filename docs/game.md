## Game Elements: Where Magic and Commerce Converge

Welcome to the enchanting world of Mystical Mastery, where the intricate interplay of magic and commerce shapes your adventure. Explore the fundamental elements that form the core of your experience:

### 1. Skill Categories and Tokens

Immerse yourself in six distinct skill categories, each represented by a vibrant color that reflects its magical prowess. The categories include Red, Blue, Violet, and more, embodying unique aspects of magical ability. Players earn tokens of varying values (common, rare, and legendary) from mentors based on the difficulty of completed tasks.

### 2. Gray Tokens and Contracts

Gray tokens, known as Essence Tokens, lie at the heart of mystical transactions. These tokens serve as the universal magical currency, representing the essence of magical power. Magical mentors imbue colored tokens with their specific school of magic, and players can also manually convert colored tokens into gray ones using a dedicated spell.

### 3. Achievement Tokens

A testament to your magical accomplishments, Achievement Tokens symbolize your growth within the skill categories. These tokens come in different colors, serving as markers of your mastery and progress.

### 4. Player Contracts

Engage in the art of magical challenges through Player Contracts. As a player, you can create contracts offering either colored tokens or Essence Tokens as rewards for completing tasks. Questgivers decide the type of tokens they offer, providing flexibility and strategic opportunities.

### 5. Scores

Your journey is tracked through several scores that reflect your achievements:
- Skill Enhancement Score: Measures overall growth and progress across magical skills.
- Contract Completion Score: Tracks the value of tokens earned by successfully fulfilling contracts.
- Commodity Conversion Score: Reflects success in converting commodities into colored tokens.
- Market Success Score: Records achievements in in-game market transactions.
- Quester Score: Tallies tokens earned from contracts with other players.
- Questgiver Score: Counts tokens granted to other players via contracts.

### 6. Converting Tokens

Players have the ability to manually convert colored tokens into gray tokens (Essence Tokens) using a designated button/spell. This process occurs prior to using tokens in contracts or transforming them into commodities.

### 7. Commodities and Conversion

Discover a unique facet of the game by exchanging your Essence Tokens for fractional ownership of virtual commodities. From wheat to coffee, silver, and more, these commodities hold magical energy that can be reabsorbed into your skills through conversion.
