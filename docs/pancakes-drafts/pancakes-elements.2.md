**Game Elements:**

1. **Skill Categories and Tokens:**
   - The magical world is divided into six distinct skill categories.
   - Each category is represented by a specific color of magical tokens.
   - Tokens embody magical energy associated with their corresponding skills.

2. **Essence Tokens:**
   - Essence Tokens serve as a universal magical currency.
   - Players acquire essence tokens through successful magical accomplishments.

3. **Blank Contracts:**
   - Similar to the creation of essence tokens, players can learn a basic spell to create blank contracts.
   - Blank contracts are the foundation for magical contracts and hold the potential for magical infusion.

4. **Magical Contracts:**
   - Players discover the capability of infusing magical power into blank contracts using commodities.
   - Contracts represent magical challenges and interactions, shaped by the commodities used to imbue them.

5. **Commodities:**
   - Commodities exist only in the other world, representing different elements such as silver, cotton, and more.
   - Commodities have innate magical properties that can be harnessed for magical contracts.

6. **Turn-Based Interaction:**
   - On each turn, mentors in the magical world imbue essence tokens for the players.
   - Novices have the ability to create blank contracts, mimicking the creation of essence tokens.

7. **Experimentation and Discovery:**
   - Novice characters stumble upon the notion of infusing commodities into blank contracts.
   - This discovery leads to the creation of magical contracts that draw power from commodities.

8. **Parallel Progression:**
   - As novices experiment with commodity-contract infusion, a parallel progression emerges with the magical world's mentor-token imbuing.

9. **Magical Effects:**
   - Magical contracts, formed from commodities, offer various magical effects and challenges.
   - These effects are determined by the type of commodity used to imbue the contract.

10. **Duality and Challenges:**
    - Ethical dilemmas arise as novices navigate the consequences of their commodity-contract experimentation.
    - The magical world observes potential shifts in perceptions due to novices' actions.
