Game Rules:

1. Players earn tokens by completing task contracts. Task contracts offer rewards in different token types based on their difficulty and nature of tasks.

2. There are six colors of tokens: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and violet. Each color has three values: common, rare, and legendary.

3. Players have wallets that contain tokens of various colors and values. The values in the wallets represent the current inventory of tokens earned through task contracts and token-to-commodity conversions.

4. The game system conditionally issues tokens to players every turn through a predefined mechanism. These game-issued tokens are automatically added to the players' inventories, representing value earned through the granting mechanism.

5. Game-issued tokens contribute to a separate score called the "value score" or "issued tokens score," reflecting the total value earned through the granting mechanism.

6. The exchange rate between common tokens and the seven other asset classes is pegged to CAD, a real-world currency.

7. Players can use tokens to perform token-to-commodity conversions within their wallets, exchanging tokens for the seven other asset classes: wheat, coffee, silver, cotton, bitcoin, DJIM, and CAD.

8. Task contract rewards and token-to-commodity conversions do not affect the issued tokens score, only the current inventory of tokens in the players' wallets.

9. Players cannot trade tokens with each other directly. Instead, they create task contracts that offer token rewards or exchange tokens for commodities.

10. The values of rare and legendary tokens are influenced by player interactions and preferences. Players' choices in accepting or declining task contracts for certain tokens can impact their perceived value.

11. The game's dynamic pricing algorithm continuously analyzes player interactions and task completion rates, adjusting token values over time to maintain balance and fairness.

12. Players can strategically manage their assets, aim for rare and legendary tokens for more valuable rewards, and passively accumulate tokens through game-issued mechanisms.
