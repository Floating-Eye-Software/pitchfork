**Phase 1: The Path of Color Magic**
1. **Intermission 1: Foundation of Magic**
   - Novices enter the magical world under the guidance of mentors who provide them with colored tokens.
   - Players learn the basics of color magic and how to cast spells using different colored tokens.
   - Novices are introduced to the concept of magical schools and their associated colors.

**Phase 2: Unleashing Agency with Contracts**
2. **Intermission 2: Discovery of Contracts**
   - While exploring a hidden library, novices find an ancient tome on magical challenges and contracts.
   - Players experiment with creating and completing their first contracts, gaining a sense of agency.
   - The UI introduces the concept of contracts, allowing players to issue and accept challenges.

**Phase 3: The Essence of Commodities**
3. **Intermission 3: Unveiling Commodities**
   - Novices embark on a journey to explore the magical world's depths, discovering the essence of commodities.
   - As they delve into commodities, players learn how commodities contain their own magical essence.
   - The UI subtly introduces commodities as an additional layer of magical essence.

4. **Intermission 4: Essence of Conversion**
   - With a better understanding of commodities, novices begin experimenting with converting commodities to tokens.
   - NPC mentors shed light on the process of converting commodities and introduce fractional essence.
   - Players witness the process of converting commodities into tokens and the magical essence it entails.

**Phase 4: Unveiling the Nexus of Realms**
5. **Intermission 5: The Nexus of Realms**
   - Novices make a groundbreaking discovery: a hidden link between their world and another realm.
   - Players uncover the existence of virtual assets in the parallel realm and the concept of interdimensional economics.
   - The UI showcases the parallel realm's commodities and virtual assets in a cinematic sequence.

**Phase 5: Restoring the Harmonic Balance**
6. **Intermission 6: Restoring the Balance**
   - After their experiments lead to chaos, novices realize the urgent need to restore harmony.
   - Players adapt to the new challenges, utilizing their knowledge of color magic, contracts, commodities, and conversions.
   - The UI evolves to reflect the challenges faced, emphasizing the importance of resolution.

**Phase 6: Mastery and the Path Forward**
7. **Epilogue: Mastery and the Path Forward**
   - Through their journey, novices transform into skilled practitioners with mastery over various magic types.
   - Players come to understand the interconnectedness of color magic, contracts, commodities, and conversions.
   - The UI reflects players' progress and mastery, showcasing their achievements and the magical world's newfound stability.
