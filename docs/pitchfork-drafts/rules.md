## Gameplay Rules: Mastering Magic and Commerce

Dive into the heart of Mystical Mastery's gameplay, where the convergence of magic and commerce sets the stage for your journey. Explore the following rules that govern your actions, decisions, and progress in the mystical world:

### 1. Skill Categories and Tokens

In the mystical realm, magic is divided into six distinct skill categories, each represented by a unique color. These categories include Red, Blue, Violet, and more, each corresponding to specific magical abilities. Players earn tokens of varying values (common, rare, and legendary) from magical mentors based on the difficulty of tasks completed.

### 2. Gray Tokens and Contracts

Gray tokens, also known as Essence Tokens, are the universal currency of magical transactions. These tokens can be imbued with magical power by mentors and are used to fuel spells and create contracts. Players create contracts offering either colored tokens or Essence Tokens as rewards for completing magical challenges.

### 3. Achievement Tokens

The rewards for your magical accomplishments come in the form of Achievement Tokens. These tokens reflect your progress within specific skill categories and serve as a testament to your growth and mastery in the mystical arts.

### 4. Player Contracts

Participate in a web of magical challenges through Player Contracts. As a player, you can create contracts offering either colored tokens or Essence Tokens as rewards for completing tasks. Questgivers have the flexibility to choose the type of tokens they offer in their contracts.

### 5. Scores

Several scores track your achievements and progress within the game:
- Skill Enhancement Score: Reflects overall progress and growth across magical skills.
- Contract Completion Score: Measures the value of tokens earned through completed contracts.
- Commodity Conversion Score: Reflects the success in converting commodities into newly colored tokens.
- Market Success Score: Measures achievements in in-game market transactions.
- Quester Score: Tracks tokens earned through contracts from other players.
- Questgiver Score: Tracks tokens given to other players via contracts.

### 6. Converting Tokens

Players have the option to manually convert colored tokens into gray tokens (Essence Tokens) using a button/spell in their wallet. This process occurs before tokens are used in contracts or converted into commodities.

### 7. Commodities and Conversion

Exchange your Essence Tokens for fractional shares of virtual commodities such as wheat, coffee, silver, and more. Converting commodities back into newly colored tokens allows you to re-absorb the essence of commodities into your magical skills.
