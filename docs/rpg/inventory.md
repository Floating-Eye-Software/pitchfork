**Inventory Window Design:**

1. **Token Section:**
   - Different sized/shaped slots for each rarity to hold all tokens.
   - Players can manually drag and drop tokens between slots as desired.
   - Tokens don't automatically sort or move on their own.
   - Tokens of the same colour are stackable.

2. **Contracts Section:**
   - Blank contracts stack.
   - Player contracts are displayed individually.

3. **Commodities Section:**
   - Displays icons representing held positions/commodities.
   - Commodities can stack, and the number of shares is displayed as a badge or label.

4. **Workbench Section:**
   - Contains the blanken button and serves as a temporary holding area for items.
   - Players can move icons to the workbench for various actions before placing them back in appropriate sections.
