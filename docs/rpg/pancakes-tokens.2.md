**Game Rules:**

1. **Token Rewards:** Each turn, players receive token rewards based on their interactions and achievements. The type of token (color) is determined by the difficulty of the actions they completed. Players can receive at most one token of each color per turn.

2. **Contract Tokens:** Players can complete Task Contracts offering various tasks of different colors and difficulty levels. Successful completion earns players Contract Tokens specific to the task.

3. **Market Actions:** Players can use Wallet Tokens to perform Market Actions, exchanging tokens for commodities like wheat, coffee, silver, cotton, bitcoin, DJIM, and CAD. Exchange rates are fixed, pegged to CAD.

4. **Wallet Contents:** Players' wallets store Token Rewards, Contract Tokens, and Wallet Tokens without distinction.

5. **Task Contract Offers:** Players create Task Contracts, offering token rewards. Others can accept or decline these contracts without penalties. Declined contracts remain available for others to consider.

6. **No Direct Trading:** Players cannot directly trade tokens. Instead, they create Task Contracts with token rewards or perform Market Actions for token-to-commodity conversions.

7. **Scoring:** The score is the total value of granted token rewards, including common, rare, and legendary tokens. Rare and legendary tokens increase the score by their value in common tokens.

**Future Improvements:**

1. **Community Contracts:** Implement cooperative Task Contracts, encouraging players to work together for shared rewards.

2. **Trading Pools:** Allow players to form trading pools for collective investments in high-stakes Task Contracts or Market Actions.

3. **Leaderboards and Achievements:** Create leaderboards based on scores and achievements, offering rewards and recognition for top-performing players.

4. **Limited-Time Events:** Organize time-limited in-game events with specific objectives and unique rewards.

5. **Player-Driven Economy:** Introduce player influence on token economy, such as voting on dynamic pricing algorithm changes.

6. **Task Contract Variety:** Continuously add diverse Task Contracts with varying rewards and difficulty levels.

7. **Commodity Market Dynamics:** Create fluctuating commodity markets influenced by player interactions and events.

8. **Limited Token Supply:** Limit Token Rewards to create scarcity and strategic decisions.

9. **Player Diplomacy:** Enable negotiations, alliances, and diplomatic interactions between players.

3. **Token Economy:** How do players perceive the token economy in terms of earning and spending tokens? Are there enough strategic choices for players to make regarding token-to-commodity conversions?

4. **Future Improvements:** Which of the future improvements are most appealing to players, and do they align with the overall game vision? Are there any other potential improvements or features players would like to see?

5. **Rules Clarity:** Are there any aspects of the game rules or mechanics that still require further clarification? Are there any potential ambiguities that players might encounter?

6. **Player Feedback:** What are the players' reactions to the game mechanics and future improvements? Gathering feedback from playtesters or the game's audience can be invaluable in identifying areas for enhancement.

7. **Scoring System Impact:** How does the scoring system affect player strategies? Do players find it rewarding to aim for rare and legendary tokens to boost their scores, or is there a need for more balancing in this aspect?

8. **Player Interaction:** How are player interactions affected by the inability to trade tokens directly? Are players finding the cooperative and competitive elements satisfying?
