**Game Rules:**

1. **Tokens:**
   - Players receive tokens of different colors (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet) every turn based on the difficulty of their own interactions and achievements in the game. The type of token (common, rare, legendary) is determined by the difficulty of the tasks they completed. Tokens remain as whole numbers in the wallets and can be stacked but not divided. Each token visually represents its color and rarity.

2. **Contracts:**
   - Players can create contracts that offer tokens as rewards. The tokens offered in the contracts retain their original color and rarity. Players can exchange any tokens they receive without any restrictions.

3. **Commodities:**
   - Players can use their whole tokens to perform market actions, exchanging them for fractional shares of commodities like wheat, coffee, silver, cotton, bitcoin, DJIM, and CAD. Each commodity has its own unique color, distinct from the six token colors. The exchange rates are fixed, pegged to CAD.

4. **Conversion of Commodities to Tokens:**
   - When players convert commodities back into tokens, the new tokens will take on a color based on the commodity they originated from. These new colors will be distinct from the original six token colors, representing the essence of the commodity integrated into the tokens.

5. **Scores:**
   - **Reward Score:** The total value of all tokens granted by the game system as rewards to players.
   - **Wallet Score:** The total value of all tokens and commodities currently held by players in their wallets, including tokens earned through rewards and quests/challenges.
   - **Quester Score:** The total value of tokens received by a player from other players through contracts.
   - **Questgiver Score:** The total value of tokens given by a player to other players via contracts.
   - **Capital Gains/Losses:** The overall success of players in exchanging commodities in the market.

6. **Overall Score:**
   - The overall score is a composite measure that takes into account the other five scores (Reward Score, Wallet Score, Quester Score, Questgiver Score, and Capital Gains/Losses). It provides a comprehensive assessment of a player's performance and progress in the game.
