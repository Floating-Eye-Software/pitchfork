**Game Rules:**

1. **Tokens:**
   - Players receive tokens of different colors (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet) every turn based on the difficulty of their own interactions and achievements in the game.
   - Token rarity (common, rare, legendary) is determined by the difficulty of tasks completed. Tokens remain whole numbers and visually represent their color and rarity.

2. **Contracts and Player Interactions:**
   - Players engage with each other through contracts that offer token rewards for completing specific tasks or challenges.
   - Contracts act as bounties, motivating players to achieve objectives set by other players.
   - Questgivers independently determine token rewards for their quests, allowing for diverse and player-driven rewards.

3. **Commodities and Virtual Assets:**
   - Players can use their tokens to exchange for fractional shares of virtual assets representing real-world commodities (wheat, coffee, silver, cotton, bitcoin, DJIM, CAD).
   - Each virtual commodity has a distinct color, separate from the six token colors. Exchange rates are fixed, pegged to CAD, and based on real-world asset values.

4. **Conversion of Commodities to Tokens:**
   - Converting virtual commodities back into tokens results in new tokens with colors distinct from the original six token colors, representing the essence of the integrated commodities.

5. **Scores:**
   - **Reward Score:** The total value of all tokens granted by the game system as rewards to players.
   - **Wallet Score:** The total value of all tokens and virtual commodities currently held by players in their wallets, including tokens earned through rewards and quests/challenges.
   - **Quester Score:** The total value of tokens received by a player from other players through contracts.
   - **Questgiver Score:** The total value of tokens given by a player to other players via contracts.
   - **Capital Gains:** The overall success of players in exchanging virtual commodities in the market.

6. **Overall Score:**
   - The overall score is a composite measure that takes into account the other five scores (Reward Score, Wallet Score, Quester Score, Questgiver Score, and Capital Gains). It provides a comprehensive assessment of a player's performance and progress in the game.

**Future Improvements:**

1. **Trading Pools:** Introduce trading pools that allow players to pool their tokens and resources for mutual benefits and increased trading opportunities.
2. **Community Contracts:** Implement a system for players to collaborate and create community-based contracts, adding a cooperative element to the game.
3. **Leaderboards:** Create leaderboards for each score to showcase top players based on their achievements in different areas of the game.
4. **Achievements:** Include an achievement system to reward players for reaching milestones related to each score.
