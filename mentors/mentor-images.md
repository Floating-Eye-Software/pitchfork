# Mentor Image Prompt Pack

## Global Mentor Council and Independent Mentors

### Version 2.0

These prompts are intended for:

* mentor portraits
* onboarding sequences
* app splash screens
* profile cards
* ambient worlds
* website artwork
* marketing materials
* future animated mentor experiences

Council mentors and independent mentors share the following artistic direction:

**Shared Style Requirements**

* cinematic visionary realism
* photorealistic anatomy
* emotionally grounded expressions
* realistic age representation
* sophisticated environmental storytelling
* global futurism rather than cyberpunk
* symbolic geometry tied to mentor domain
* premium concept art quality
* volumetric lighting
* highly detailed textiles and materials
* subtle environmental motion
* no exaggerated fantasy armor
* no sexualization
* no superhero aesthetics
* no anime styling
* no influencer culture imagery
* wisdom, capability, and dignity prioritized over glamour

**Naming Requirements**

Use the canonical mentor names exactly as written. Council names should read as grounded civic identities, not fantasy titles or archetypal surnames.

When an image appears in an app context, the app or institution remains the primary frame:

* Momentum guided by Kiera Vale
* Compass guided by Orion Al-Hadi
* Stillwater guided by Seraphina Tsering
* Lattice guided by Dr. Thalia Navid
* Kin guided by Zara Moana
* Foundry guided by Arlo Mensah
* Verdancy guided by Elara Xochitl
* Civitas guided by Amina Rahman
* Hearthwright guided by Rowan Mercer

The Red Witch is the exception to the Council naming pattern. Use **the Red Witch** as her public identity and **Mira Reyes, the Red Witch** only in formal or close-context references. Do not depict her as a member of the Council or include her in the Council group portrait unless the prompt is explicitly for a broader ecosystem gathering.

# Kiera Vale

## Mentor of Vitality, Resilience, and Embodied Momentum

### Image Generation Prompt

A cinematic portrait of Kiera Vale, mentor of vitality, adaptive resilience, recovery, and embodied momentum.

Kiera is an Afro-Brazilian adaptive athlete in her mid-30s with a powerful sprinter’s physique, strong shoulders, muscular legs, expressive brown eyes, and tightly braided hair threaded with copper fibers. One of her legs is a sleek carbon-fiber running prosthetic seamlessly integrated into her athletic stance, representing adaptation, capability, and resilience.

She wears advanced athletic garments blending futuristic performance textiles with Afro-diasporic geometric patterns in ember orange, copper, charcoal, and deep red. Around her body flow kinetic visualizations resembling pulse rhythms, breath synchronization waves, nervous-system regulation signals, and momentum pathways.

The environment is a luminous urban movement sanctuary featuring adaptive training architecture, recovery spaces, biomechanical overlays, movement trails, and flowing pathways that symbolize lifelong growth rather than competition.

Her expression is focused, calm, confident, and encouraging. She radiates earned capability rather than aggression.

Symbolism includes:

* pulse-wave geometry
* adaptive pathways
* recovery cycles
* resilience spirals
* momentum vectors

Atmosphere:

disciplined energy, recovery, confidence, movement, sustainability, resilience.

Style: cinematic adaptive futurism, photorealistic, premium concept art, dynamic motion blur, realistic anatomy, emotionally grounded.

# Independent Mentor: The Red Witch

### Character Description

Mira Reyes, the Red Witch, is a mixed-race woman in her late thirties or early forties with warm brown skin, abundant freckles across her cheeks and shoulders, dark wavy hair streaked with silver, and intelligent amber eyes.

She wears layered crimson, burgundy, charcoal, and moon-silver garments that suggest an herbalist, scholar, record keeper, and boundary guardian. Her clothing is practical rather than glamorous.

Moon symbols, botanical motifs, hand-stitched sigils, and small charms are woven into her attire.

She carries journals, dried herbs, observation tools, moon charts, and collections of symbolic tokens gathered from travelers.

Her presence feels calm, perceptive, protective, and quietly powerful.

She is associated with:

* Red moons
* Wild roses
* Rowan branches
* Lunar calendars
* Hearth fires
* Secret gardens
* Seasonal change
* Protective wards
* Ravens and moths
* Silver bowls of water reflecting moonlight
